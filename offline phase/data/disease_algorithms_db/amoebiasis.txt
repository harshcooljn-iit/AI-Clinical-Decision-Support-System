DISEASE: Amoebiasis (Intestinal)

ABBREVIATIONS:
- TDS: Three times a day
- PO: Per os (by mouth)
- mg: Milligram
- kg: Kilogram
- Tab: Tablet

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Demonstration of cysts and/or trophozoites of _Entamoeba histolytica_ in the stool.

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF Patient is an asymptomatic cyst passer
  THEN Administer Tab. diloxanide furoate 500 mg TDS for 10 days
ELSE IF Patient has acute amoebic dysentery or chronic infection
  THEN
    IF Patient is an adult
      THEN Administer Tab. metronidazole 400 – 800 mg 8 hourly PO for 10 days
    ELSE IF Patient is a child
      THEN Administer metronidazole 15 mg/kg divided in three doses for 7 days
    ELSE IF Patient is an adult
      THEN Administer Tab. tinidazole 2 g orally as single dose
    ELSE IF Patient is a child
      THEN Administer tinidazole 50 mg/kg/day in three divided doses for 10 days
  ENDIF
  Followed by Tab. diloxanide furoate 500 mg 8 hourly for 10 days
ENDIF
