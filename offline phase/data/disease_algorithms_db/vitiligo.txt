DISEASE: Vitiligo

ABBREVIATIONS:
- PUVA: Psoralen plus Ultraviolet A radiation
- mg: milligram
- kg: kilogram
- Tab.: Tablet

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Clinical examination based on characteristic asymptomatic depigmented lesions.

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF Non-pharmacological treatment is sufficient THEN
    - Provide counselling and reassurance.
    - Advise good general health and a balanced nutritious diet enriched with adequate proteins, vitamin B complex, vitamin E, minerals such as copper, zinc, and iron.
    - Advise avoiding trauma.
    - Advise avoiding soaps and detergents containing phenolic compounds and rubber goods.
ELSE
    IF Pharmacological treatment is indicated THEN
        IF Topical steroids are chosen THEN
            - Prescribe mometasone furoate 0.1% cream.
            - OR Prescribe betamethasone valerate 0.05% cream or ointment.
        ELSE IF Topical calcineurin inhibitors are chosen THEN
            - Prescribe tacrolimus 0.03% ointment.
            - OR Prescribe tacrolimus 0.1% ointment.
        ELSE IF PUVA therapy is chosen THEN
            - Administer oral methoxsalen (0.6 mg/kg).
            - OR Apply topical psoralens followed by sun exposure.
        ENDIF
    ENDIF
ENDIF

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- (None specified in the provided text for the listed treatments)
