DISEASE: Status Epilepticus

ABBREVIATIONS:
- Inj.: Injection
- mg: Milligram
- kg: Kilogram
- max: Maximum
- iv: Intravenous
- im: Intramuscular
- NS: Normal Saline
- AED: Antiepileptic Drug
- LFT: Liver Function Test
- RFT: Renal Function Test
- CRP: C-Reactive Protein
- PE: Prodrug Equivalent
- AED: Antiepileptic Drug

DIAGNOSTICS:
- A single epileptic seizure lasting >30 minutes.
- OR a series of epileptic seizures during which consciousness is not regained between ictal events in a >30 minute period.

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF Status Epilepticus is diagnosed THEN

    // Initial Treatment (0-10 minutes)
    IF IV access is available THEN
        Administer Inj. Lorazepam 0.1 mg/kg (max 4 mg) at a rate of 2 mg/min.
    ELSE IF IV access is not available THEN
        Administer Inj. Midazolam 0.15-0.2 mg/kg iv/im (max 5 mg).
    ENDIF

    IF IV access is not available THEN
        Administer Buccal Midazolam 0.3 mg/kg (max 5 mg).
    ENDIF

    IF IV access is not available AND rectal access is available THEN
        Administer Per Rectal Diazepam 0.5 mg/kg (max 10 mg).
    ENDIF

    Establish airway, breathing, circulation.
    Monitor cardiorespiratory status / oxygenation.
    Perform Investigations: Glucose, sodium, potassium, Calcium, magnesium, CRP, AED level, LFT, RFT.

    // Second-line Treatment (After 10 minutes if no response to initial treatment)
    IF No response after 10 minutes THEN
        Administer Inj. Phenytoin 20 mg/kg in NS (max 1000mg) at a rate of 1 mg/kg/min.
    ENDIF

    // Third-line Treatment (If no response to Phenytoin/Fosphenytoin)
    IF No response after Phenytoin/Fosphenytoin administration (refractory status) THEN
        Repeat Inj. Phenytoin 20 mg/kg in NS (max 1000mg) at a rate of 1 mg/kg/min.
    ENDIF

    // Fourth-line Treatment (At 25-30 minutes if no response to previous treatments)
    IF No response after 25-30 minutes THEN
        Administer Inj. Valproate 20 mg/kg iv at a rate of 6 mg/kg/min OR
        Administer Inj. Phenobarbitone 20 mg/kg iv at a rate of 1.5 mg/kg/min.
    ENDIF

    // Fifth-line Treatment (If no response after 35-45 minutes)
    IF No response after 35-45 minutes THEN
        Administer Inj. Phenobarbitone 10 mg/kg iv.
    ENDIF

ENDIF
