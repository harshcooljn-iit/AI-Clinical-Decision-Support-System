- Clinical findings (uterine size, os status, discharge, pain)
- Ultrasonography (USG) findings (fetal cardiac activity, gestational sac location, uterine contents)
- Human Chorionic Gonadotropin (hCG) levels (qualitative and quantitative)
- Bleeding time, clotting time, Clotting Reaction Time (CRT) in specific cases (e.g., missed abortion)
