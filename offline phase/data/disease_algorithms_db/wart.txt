1.  DISEASE: Wart
2.  ABBREVIATIONS:
    *   TCA: Trichloroacetic acid
    *   Podophyllotoxin: Podophyllotoxin
    *   Podophyllin: Podophyllin
3.  DIAGNOSTICS: Clinical examination and/or biopsy.
4.  TREATMENT ALGORITHM:
    *   IF the wart is being treated non-pharmacologically:
        *   THEN advise the patient to avoid scratching.
        *   THEN advise the patient to avoid shared towels, contact sports, and communal bathing.
        *   THEN advise safe sex practice if genital warts are present.
    *   IF the wart is being treated pharmacologically:
        *   THEN consider Podophyllotoxin 0.5% solution or 0.15% cream.
            *   IF Podophyllotoxin 0.5% solution or 0.15% cream is chosen:
                *   THEN apply twice daily for 3 days.
                *   THEN follow with 4 days of rest.
                *   THEN repeat the 3-day application and 4-day rest cycle for a total of 3 cycles.
        *   THEN consider Cryotherapy.
        *   THEN consider Trichloroacetic acid (TCA) application.
        *   THEN consider Podophyllin 10%-25%.
5.  TABULAR DOSAGES: Not applicable in this guideline.
6.  CONTRAINDICATIONS: Not specified in this guideline.
