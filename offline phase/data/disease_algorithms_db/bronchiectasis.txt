- ACD: Autogenic drainage
- AD: Autogenic drainage
- ACBT: Active Cycle of Breathing Technique
- FET: Forced Expiratory Technique

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Clinical features (persistent cough, copious mucopurulent sputum).
- Radiological presentation (plain chest x-ray, HRCT thorax).
- HRCT findings: Direct signs (bronchial dilatation, increased bronchoarterial ratio, lack of airway tapering, airway visibility within 1 cm of costal pleura/fissures), Indirect signs (bronchial wall thickening, mucoid impaction, bronchiolitis/tree-in-bud nodules, mosaic attenuation, bronchial artery hyperplasia).
