DISEASE: Angina Pectoris

ABBREVIATIONS:
- mg: milligram
- BD: bis in die (twice a day)
- OD: omne die (once a day)
- LVH: Left Ventricular Hypertrophy
- Tab: Tablet
- Sublingual: Administered under the tongue

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Precordial chest pain due to stress or exertion.
- Associated symptoms: palpitation, breathlessness.
- Symptoms typically associated with exertion and emotional upset.
- Symptoms relieved quickly by rest and/or sublingual nitrates.
- ECG findings: ST depression, T wave inversion, LVH.

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

// Initial Management of Acute Angina Attack
IF acute angina attack occurs THEN
    ADMINISTER Tab. isosorbide dinitrate 5 mg sublingual.
    REPEAT dose as required.
    OR
    ADMINISTER Tab. nitroglycerine 0.3-0.6 mg sublingual.
ENDIF

// Chronic Prophylaxis Indication
IF angina attack occurs twice in a week THEN
    RECOMMEND chronic prophylaxis.
ENDIF

// First-line Chronic Prophylaxis
IF chronic prophylaxis is recommended THEN
    ADMINISTER Tab. metoprolol 50 mg BD.
    OR
    ADMINISTER Tab. atenolol 50 mg OD.
ENDIF

// Second-line Chronic Prophylaxis (if Beta Blockers contraindicated or ineffective)
IF beta blockers are contraindicated OR angina persists despite beta blockers THEN
    ADMINISTER Tab. diltiazem 60-120 mg/day.
ENDIF

// Antiplatelet Therapy
ADMINISTER Tab. aspirin 100-150 mg/day.
OR
ADMINISTER Tab. clopidogrel 75 mg/day.

// Non-pharmacological Treatment
IF patient has Angina Pectoris THEN
    ADVISE to avoid heavy exertion.
    ADVISE to take rest during acute stage.
    ADVISE to stop smoking.
    ADVISE weight reduction.
ENDIF

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
// Beta Blocker Contraindications (Implied - specific contraindications not listed in text)
IF patient has bradycardia, heart block, severe asthma, or decompensated heart failure THEN
    DO NOT administer Tab. metoprolol or Tab. atenolol.
ENDIF

// Diltiazem Contraindications (Implied - specific contraindications not listed in text)
IF patient has hypotension, sick sinus syndrome, or 2nd/3rd degree AV block THEN
    DO NOT administer Tab. diltiazem.
ENDIF
