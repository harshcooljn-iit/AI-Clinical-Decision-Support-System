```text
DISEASE: Hookworm Infestation

ABBREVIATIONS:
- BD: Twice daily
- mg: Milligram
- kg: Kilogram
- Tab: Tablet
- Susp: Suspension

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Asymptomatic or symptoms of anemia (Hypochromic microcytic).
- Pruritic maculopapular dermatitis (ground itch).
- Mild transient pneumonitis.
- Intestinal manifestations (epigastric pain, inflammatory diarrhea).
- Major consequences (progressive iron deficiency anemia, hypoproteinemia).

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF Patient Age >= 2 years THEN
    IF Drug is Mebendazole THEN
        Administer Tab. mebendazole 100mg BD for 3 days.
    ELSE IF Drug is Pyrantel Pamoate THEN
        Administer Tab. pyrantel pamoate 250mg once daily for 3 days.
    ELSE IF Drug is Albendazole THEN
        Administer Tab albendazole 400mg as a single dose.
    ENDIF
ELSE IF Patient Age >= 1 year AND Patient Age < 2 years THEN
    IF Drug is Pyrantel Pamoate THEN
        Administer Susp. pyrantel pamoate 10mg/kg as a single dose.
    ELSE IF Drug is Albendazole THEN
        Administer Syrup albendazole 200mg as a single dose.
    ENDIF
ELSE IF Patient Age < 1 year THEN
    IF Drug is Pyrantel Pamoate THEN
        Administer Susp. pyrantel pamoate 10mg/kg as a single dose.
    ELSE IF Drug is Albendazole THEN
        Administer Syrup albendazole 200mg as a single dose.
    ENDIF
ENDIF

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- Mebendazole: Contraindicated in children less than 2 years of age.
- Pyrantel Pamoate: Not used in children below 1 year of age.
```
