```text
DISEASE: Tooth Maxillary Fracture

ABBREVIATIONS:
TMJ: Temporomandibular Joint

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Pain, mobility of fractured segment of tooth or bone
- Step deformity
- Painful or restricted TMJ movement
- Bleeding from traumatic site
- Radiographs for fracture suspected sites

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:
IF (Patient presents with signs/symptoms of tooth or maxillary bone fracture) THEN
    IF (Bleeding is present) THEN
        Perform primary treatment for bleeding
    ENDIF
    IF (Pain is present) THEN
        Perform primary treatment for pain
    ENDIF
    IF (Soft tissue trauma is present) THEN
        Perform primary sutures for soft tissue trauma
    ENDIF
    IF (Fracture is suspected) THEN
        Obtain radiographs of suspected fracture sites
    ENDIF
    IF (Bone fracture is confirmed or strongly suspected) THEN
        Refer patient to an oral maxilla facial surgeon
    ENDIF
ENDIF
```
