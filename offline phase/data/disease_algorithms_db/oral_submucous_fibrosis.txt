DISEASE: Oral Submucous Fibrosis

ABBREVIATIONS:
- OSMF: Oral Submucous Fibrosis

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Salient features include:
    - Gradually reduced mouth opening (Trismus)
    - Blanching (White opaque) oral mucosa
    - Recurrent ulcers & burning sensations on taking spicy food
    - Change of voice, difficulty in deglutition
    - Poor oral hygiene with gingivitis & periodontitis
    - Small shrunken uvula and decreased mobility of soft palate

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:
IF patient has Oral Submucous Fibrosis (OSMF) THEN
    IF patient uses tobacco, lime, or Betelnut THEN
        THEN advise to quit habit as soon as possible
    ENDIF
    THEN advise mouth opening exercises (physical exercises)
    THEN advise Oral Hygiene prophylaxis
    THEN consider local medicaments in form of steroids
ENDIF
