```text
DISEASE: Enterobiasis

ABBREVIATIONS:
    _Enterobius vermicularis_: Pin worm
    mg: milligram
    kg: kilogram
    Syr.: Syrup

DIAGNOSTICS:
    Demonstration of _Enterobius vermicularis_ ova in perianal swabs or on cellophane tape pressed against perianal skin.

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF Patient is an adult or child > 2 years old:
    THEN
        IF Treatment choice is Mebendazole:
            THEN Administer Mebendazole 100 mg as a single dose.
        ELSE IF Treatment choice is Pyrantel Pamoate:
            THEN Administer Pyrantel Pamoate 11 mg/kg body weight as a single dose.
        ELSE IF Treatment choice is Albendazole:
            THEN Administer Albendazole 400 mg as a single dose.
    ENDIF
ELSE IF Patient is a child 1-2 years old:
    THEN Administer Albendazole Syrup 200 mg as a single dose.
ENDIF

Repeat treatment after two weeks.

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
    Mebendazole is contraindicated in pregnancy and in children below one year of age.
```
