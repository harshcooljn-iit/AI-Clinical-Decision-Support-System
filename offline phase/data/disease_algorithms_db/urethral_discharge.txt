DISEASE: Urethral Discharge

ABBREVIATIONS:
- NACO: National AIDS Control Organisation
- IM: Intramuscular
- TDS: Three times a day
- BD: Twice a day
- ICTC: Integrated Counseling and Testing Centre
- stat: Immediately

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Clinical examination
- Gram stain
- Culture

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF the patient presents with Urethral Discharge, then:
    - Educate and counsel the client and sexual partner regarding safer sex practices and the importance of completing treatment.
    - Treat the patient's partner.
    - Advise sexual abstinence or condom use during treatment.
    - Provide condoms and educate on correct/consistent use.
    - Refer the patient to ICTC.
    - Schedule follow-up after 7 days.
    - Schedule follow-up on the 3rd, 7th, and 14th day for lower abdominal pain.
    - Schedule follow-up on the 7th, 14th, and 21st day for inguinal bubo.
    - If symptoms persist, re-assess for re-infection and advise prompt referral.
    - Consider immunization against Hepatitis B.

    IF the patient has Urethral Discharge, then administer Kit 1 provided by NACO:
        - Tab. azithromycin 1g stat
        - Tab. cefixime 400 mg stat

    IF the patient has Vaginal discharge, then administer Kit 2 provided by NACO:
        - Tab. secnidazole 2g stat
        - Cap. fluconazole 150mg stat

    IF the patient has Genital ulcerative disease (non herpetic), then administer Kit 3 provided by NACO:
        - Inj. benzathine penicillin 2.4 mu IM stat
        - Tab. azithromycin 1g stat

    IF the patient has Genital ulcerative disease (non herpetic), then administer Kit 4 provided by NACO:
        - Cap. doxycycline 100mg BD for 15 days
        - Tab. azithromycin 1g stat

    IF the patient has Genital ulcerative disease (herpetic), then administer Kit 5 provided by NACO:
        - Tab. acyclovir 400mg TDS for 7 days

    IF the patient has Lower abdominal pain (lap), then administer Kit 6 provided by NACO:
        - Tab. cefixime 400mg stat
        - Tab. metronidazole 400mg BD for 15 days
        - Cap. doxycycline 100mg BD for 14 days

    IF the patient has Inguinal bubo, then administer Kit 7 provided by NACO:
        - Cap. doxycycline 100mg BD for 21 days
        - Tab. azithromycin 1g stat

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
