```text
DISEASE: Abdominal Injury
ABBREVIATIONS:
    - USG: Ultrasound
    - CECT: Contrast Enhanced Computed Tomography
    - TPRBP: Temperature, Pulse, Respiratory Rate, Blood Pressure
    - AG: Abdominal Girth

DIAGNOSTICS:
    - History (assault, RTA, falls)
    - Haemoglobin levels, hematocrit
    - X-ray abdomen standing
    - USG- abdomen
    - CECT- abdomen and pelvis

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:
    IF Patient has blunt abdominal injury THEN
        - Resuscitate the patient.
        - Secure airway.
        - Maintain breathing.
        - Maintain circulation.
        - Arrange for compatible blood.
        - Chart TPRBP.
        - Chart AG.

        IF Vitals are unstable THEN
            - Perform emergency exploratory laparotomy.
            - Manage according to organ involved.
        ELSE IF Vitals are stable THEN
            - Review with CECT-abdomen and pelvis.
            - Manage according to organs involved and grade of injury.
        ENDIF
    ENDIF
```
