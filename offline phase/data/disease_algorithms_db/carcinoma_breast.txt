DISEASE: Carcinoma Breast

ABBREVIATIONS:
- FNAC: Fine Needle Aspiration Cytology
- DCIS: Ductal Carcinoma In Situ
- ER: Estrogen Receptor
- PR: Progesterone Receptor
- HER 2/neu: Human Epidermal growth factor Receptor 2
- IV: Intravenous
- sqm: square meter
- CMF: Cyclophosphamide, Methotrexate, 5-Fluorouracil
- CAF: Cyclophosphamide, Adriamycin, 5-Fluorouracil
- Tab: Tablet
- mg: milligram
- orally: by mouth
- daily: once a day

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Clinical presentation: Middle-aged female, lump in breast/axilla, skin changes, nipple discharge, weight loss.
- Physical examination: Breast, nipple-areola complex, axilla, opposite breast/axilla.
- Imaging: Mammography.
- Biopsy: FNAC, true cut biopsy, excisional biopsy (mass < 4 cm), incisional biopsy (mass > 4 cm).

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

SURGICAL TREATMENT:
IF Stage is IIB or IIIA THEN
  Perform Breast Conservative Surgery (Lumpectomy/quadrantectomy/wide excision) followed by radiotherapy.
ELSE IF Stage is not IIB or IIIA THEN
  Perform Modified Radical Mastectomy (removal of whole breast tissue, nipple-areola complex, overlying skin, pectoral fascia, axillary dissection).
ENDIF

IF Tumor size is < 4 cm AND solitary lump AND does not involve nipple-areola complex THEN
  Lumpectomy/quadrantectomy/wide excision is indicated.
ENDIF

IF Stage is I or IIA AND diagnosis is DCIS THEN
  Treatment is indicated.
ENDIF

PHARMACOLOGICAL TREATMENT:

NEOADJUVANT CHEMOTHERAPY:
IF Stage is IIIB (locally advanced breast cancer) THEN
  Administer chemotherapy before surgery (Neo adjuvant chemotherapy) for down staging.
ENDIF

ADJUVANT CHEMOTHERAPY:
IF any of the following conditions are met THEN
  - Node positive cancer
  - Node negative cancer with tumor size > 1 cm
  - Node negative cancer with tumor size > 0.5 cm AND adverse prognostic factors (lymphovascular invasion, high nuclear grade, high histological grade, HER 2/neu overexpression, negative hormone receptor status)
THEN
  Administer post-operative chemotherapy (Adjuvant chemotherapy).
ENDIF

CHEMOTHERAPY REGIMENS:
- CMF regimen: Cyclophosphamide, Methotrexate, 5-Fluorouracil - 6 cycles, every 21 days.
- CAF regimen: Cyclophosphamide, Adriamycin, 5-Fluorouracil - 6 cycles, every 21 days.

INDIVIDUAL DRUG DOSAGES (based on body surface area - BSA):
- Cyclophosphamide: 600 mg/sqm body area IV.
- 5 Fluorouracil: 600 mg/sqm body area IV.
- Methotrexate: 40 mg/sqm body area IV.
- Adriamycin: 50 mg/sqm body area IV.

HORMONAL REGIMEN:
IF Patient is ER/PR positive THEN
  Administer Tab. tamoxifen 20 mg orally daily for 5 years.
ENDIF

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- Tamoxifen: Contraindicated in patients who are ER/PR negative.
