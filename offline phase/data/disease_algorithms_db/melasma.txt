```text
DISEASE: Melasma

ABBREVIATIONS:
- HQ: Hydroquinone
- OCP: Oral Contraceptives
- HRT: Hormone Replacement Therapy
- Woods lamp: Wood's lamp examination

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Clinical examination
- Wood's lamp examination

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF Patient is pregnant with melasma:
    THEN Reassure the patient.
    AND Advise avoidance of sun exposure.
    AND Advise avoidance of causative factors (e.g., hormonal therapy).
ELSE:
    IF Patient is not pregnant:
        THEN Consider pharmacological treatment options.
        AND Advise photoprotection.

        IF Pharmacological treatment is chosen:
            THEN Choose one of the following topical agents:
                IF Hydroquinone is chosen:
                    THEN Prescribe Hydroquinone 2-5% cream.
                ELSE IF Azelaic acid is chosen:
                    THEN Prescribe Azelaic acid 10-20% cream.
                ELSE IF Glycolic acid is chosen:
                    THEN Prescribe Glycolic acid 6-12% cream.
                ELSE IF Hydroquinone + Tretinoin + Steroid combination is chosen:
                    THEN Prescribe Hydroquinone + Tretinoin + Steroid (Fluticasone, Mometasone, or Fluocinolone) cream.

        AND Advise avoidance of sun exposure.
        AND Advise avoidance of causative factors (e.g., OCP, HRT).
```
