```text
DISEASE: Hiv And Aids

ABBREVIATIONS:
- HIV: Human immunodeficiency virus
- AIDS: Acquired Immunodeficiency Syndrome
- ART: Antiretroviral therapy
- PGL: Persistent generalized lymphadenopathy
- TB: Tuberculosis
- ATT: Anti-tuberculosis treatment
- HBV: Hepatitis B Virus
- HCV: Hepatitis C Virus
- NACO: National AIDS Control Organization
- Hb: Hemoglobin
- TDF: Tenofovir
- 3TC: Lamivudine
- ATV/r: Atazanavir/ritonavir
- gm/dl: grams per deciliter
- cu.mm: cubic millimeters

DIAGNOSTICS:
- HIV serology
- CD4 + T lymphocytes counts (if available)
- Complete blood count and chemistry profile
- Pregnancy test
- Chest x-ray
- Urine examination
- HCV and HBV serology (depending on availability)
- Confirmation of diagnosis by trained technician in a diagnostic laboratory. Repeat test if doubt exists.

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

1.  **Assess HIV Infection Status:**
    IF HIV infection is suspected or confirmed, proceed to staging and treatment initiation criteria.

2.  **Determine WHO Clinical Stage and CD4 Count:**
    IF WHO Clinical Stage is I or II AND CD4 count is < 350 cells/cu.mm:
        THEN Initiate ART.
    ELSE IF WHO Clinical Stage is III or IV:
        THEN Initiate ART irrespective of CD4 count.
    ELSE:
        THEN Do not initiate ART based on these criteria alone.

3.  **Consider Pregnancy:**
    IF Patient is pregnant:
        THEN Initiate ART based on the same criteria as non-pregnant adults/adolescents (Clinical Stage I/II & CD4 < 350, or Stage III/IV).
        IF CD4 count is > 250 cells/cu.mm AND Nevirapine is used:
            THEN Strict monitoring for adverse effects of Nevirapine is needed.
        AVOID Efavirenz during the first trimester.

4.  **Consider TB Co-infection:**
    IF Patient has TB co-infection:
        IF Type of Tuberculosis is Pulmonary TB (Stage III):
            THEN Start ART irrespective of clinical stage and CD4 count.
            Start ATT first.
            Start ART as soon as TB treatment is tolerated after 2 weeks & before 2 months.
        ELSE IF Type of Tuberculosis is Extra pulmonary TB (Stage IV):
            THEN Start ART irrespective of clinical stage and CD4 count.
            Start ATT first.
            Start ART as soon as TB treatment is tolerated after 2 weeks & before 2 months.

5.  **Consider HBV or HCV Co-infection:**
    IF Patient has HIV-HBV or HIV-HCV co-infection:
        IF There is no evidence of chronic active Hepatitis:
            IF WHO Clinical Stage is I or II AND CD4 count is < 350 cells/cu.mm:
                THEN Start ART at CD4 Count < 350.
            ELSE IF WHO Clinical Stage is III or IV:
                THEN Start ART irrespective of CD4 Count.
        ELSE IF There is documented evidence of chronic active Hepatitis:
            THEN Start ART irrespective of any clinical stage and any CD4 count.

6.  **Select First-line ART Regimen:**
    IF Patient has Hb > 9 gm/dl:
        IF Regimen I is chosen:
            THEN Prescribe Zidovudine + Lamivudine + Nevirapine.
        ELSE IF Regimen II is chosen:
            THEN Prescribe Zidovudine + Lamivudine + Efavirenz.
    ELSE IF Patient has Hb < 9 gm/dl:
        IF Regimen I is chosen:
            THEN Prescribe Tenofovir + Lamivudine + Nevirapine.
        ELSE IF Regimen II is chosen:
            THEN Prescribe Tenofovir + Lamivudine + Efavirenz.
    IF Patient is on anti-tuberculosis treatment:
        IF Hb > 9 gm/dl:
            THEN Preferred regimen is Zidovudine + Lamivudine + Efavirenz.
        ELSE IF Hb < 9 gm/dl:
            THEN Preferred regimen is Tenofovir + Lamivudine + Efavirenz.

7.  **Select Second-line ART Regimen (if applicable):**
    IF Second-line ART is indicated:
        THEN Prescribe:
        - TDF + 3TC: Fixed dose combination of TDF 300 mg + 3TC 300 mg, taken once daily at night (0-0-1).
        - ATV/r: Cap. atazanavir 300 mg taken once daily at night (0-0-1) AND Tab. ritonavir 100 mg taken once daily at night (0-0-1).

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- Efavirenz: Avoid during the first trimester of pregnancy.
- Nevirapine: Strict monitoring for adverse effects if CD4 count is > 250 cells/cu.mm.
- Zidovudine: Regimen I and II are preferred for patients with Hb > 9 gm/dl. Regimen I (a) and II (a) are used for patients with Hb < 9 gm/dl.
```
