```text
DISEASE: Otomycosis

ABBREVIATIONS:
- KOH: Potassium Hydroxide
- Tab: Tablet
- mg: Milligram
- kg: Kilogram

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Direct otoscopic examination
- Microscopic examination in 10% KOH
- Culture in Sabouraud's Dextrose agar

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF Patient has Otomycosis THEN
    Perform ear toilet (suction/dry mopping/instrumentation).
    Administer topical clotrimazole:
        IF Formulation is powder OR liquid THEN
            IF Concentration is 1% THEN
                Apply 3-4 times a day.
                Continue for at least 1 week after clinical resolution.
            ENDIF
        ENDIF
    Administer Tab. ibuprofen:
        IF Patient is adult THEN
            Administer 400 mg as needed.
        ELSEIF Patient is child THEN
            Administer 20 mg/kg/day in 3 divided doses.
        ENDIF
    Provide patient education: Keep ear dry; prevent water entry.
ENDIF

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- (None specified in the provided text for Clotrimazole or Ibuprofen in this context, but standard contraindications apply based on drug monographs).
```
