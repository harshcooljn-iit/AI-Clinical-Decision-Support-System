DISEASE: Ventricular Tachycardia

ABBREVIATIONS:
- VT: Ventricular Tachycardia
- IV: Intravenous
- mg: Milligram
- kg: Kilogram
- TDS: Three times a day
- ICD: Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillator
- Inj.: Injection

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Runs of three or more consecutive idioventricular beats exceeding a rate of 100 beats per minute.
- Types: Sustained (> 30 seconds), Non-sustained (3 beats to 30 seconds), Monomorphic (uniform configuration), Polymorphic (varying configuration).

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF Patient has non-sustained VT AND no organic cardiac disease AND is asymptomatic:
    THEN No treatment needed.
ELSE IF Patient has sustained VT AND no organic cardiac disease AND is symptomatic:
    THEN Administer Tab. amiodarone 200-400 mg /day TDS.
ELSE IF Patient has VT with organic cardiac disease AND is haemodynamically unstable:
    THEN Administer unsynchronized cardioversion/defibrillation.
ELSE IF Patient has VT with organic cardiac disease AND is haemodynamically stable:
    THEN
        IF Inj. lidocaine is administered:
            THEN Administer 1 mg/kg IV bolus.
            THEN Repeat 0.5-1 mg/kg boluses every 5 minutes if needed.
            THEN Follow with 2-4 mg/min maintenance Infusion.
        ELSE IF Inj. amiodarone is administered:
            THEN Administer Inj. amiodarone.
        ELSE IF Lidocaine and Amiodarone fail:
            THEN Perform overdrive pacing.
    ENDIF
ENDIF
