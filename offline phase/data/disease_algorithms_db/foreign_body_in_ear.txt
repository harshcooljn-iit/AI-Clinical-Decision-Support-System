```text
DISEASE: Foreign Body In Ear

ABBREVIATIONS:
- None

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Clinical examination revealing a foreign object within the external auditory canal.

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:
IF the foreign body is an insect:
    THEN immobilize the insect using oil.
    THEN remove the insect with forceps.
ELSE IF the foreign body is a vegetable matter (e.g., seeds):
    THEN remove the foreign body with a foreign body hook.
    THEN do NOT use syringing.
ELSE IF the foreign body is non-organic (e.g., stones, beads, cotton buds, match sticks):
    THEN remove the foreign body with syringing.
    THEN remove the foreign body with a foreign body hook.
ELSE IF the foreign body is impacted:
    THEN remove the foreign body under general anesthesia.
    THEN use a microscope during removal.
```
