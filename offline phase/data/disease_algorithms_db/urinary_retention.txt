DISEASE: Urinary Retention

ABBREVIATIONS:
- BPH: Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia
- IFT: Infant Feeding Tube
- STD: Sexually Transmitted Disease
- SPC: Suprapubic catheterisation
- F: French (catheter size)

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Inability to pass urine
- Thinning of urinary stream
- Dribbling of urine
- Pain in infraumbilical region
- Palpable urinary bladder above pubic symphysis

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:
IF Urinary Retention is diagnosed THEN
    - Option 1: Insert a low diameter (10 to 12 French) urinary catheter.
    - Option 2: Insert an Infant Feeding Tube (IFT, 5 to 7 French diameter).
    - Option 3: Perform Suprapubic catheterisation (SPC).
ENDIF
