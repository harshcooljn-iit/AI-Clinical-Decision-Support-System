```text
DISEASE: Acute Parotitis

ABBREVIATIONS:
- MMR: Measles Mumps Rubella

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Clinical presentation: Bilateral parotid swelling, pain over gland, ear pain, low-grade fever, arthralgia, malaise, headache.
- Etiology: Caused by paramyxovirus.
- Epidemiology: Usually affects children less than 15 years.

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF patient has Acute Parotitis THEN
    // Non-pharmacological treatment
    THEN implement supportive measures:
        IF supportive measures needed THEN
            THEN provide bed rest
            THEN provide adequate hydration
            THEN provide antipyretics (for fever)
    // Prevention
    THEN recommend prevention:
        IF patient is susceptible THEN
            THEN recommend Measles Mumps Rubella (MMR) vaccine
ENDIF
```
