```text
DISEASE: Filariasis

ABBREVIATIONS:
- DEC: Diethylcarbamazine
- mg: Milligram
- kg: Kilogram

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Eosinophilia on total and differential count.
- Microfilaria seen in nocturnal peripheral blood smears.

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:
IF acute presentation THEN
    ADMINISTER DEC 6mg/kg daily for 21 days.
    ADMINISTER antipyretics for fever.
ELSE IF chronic presentation (lymphatic obstruction, massive lower limb oedema, etc.) THEN
    CONSIDER non-pharmacological treatment:
        - Manual lymphoedema drainage.
        - Multilayer lymphoedema bandage.
        - Compression garments.
        - Exercise.
        - Skin care (3% benzoic acid for athlete foot, paraffin ointment for dry skin, 5% salicylic acid for hyperkeratosis).
    CONSIDER surgical treatment:
        - Bypass procedures.
        - Omental pedicle.
        - Nodovenous shunt.
        - Limb reduction procedures.
ELSE
    NO SPECIFIC PHARMACOLOGICAL TREATMENT MENTIONED.
ENDIF

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- No specific contraindications mentioned for DEC in this text.
```
