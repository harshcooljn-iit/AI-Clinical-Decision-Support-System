DISEASE: Scorpion Bite

ABBREVIATIONS:
- WHO: World Health Organization
- IV: Intravenous

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Clinical presentation (pain, local signs, systemic symptoms).
- Geographic location (endemic area).
- Species identification (if possible).

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF patient presents with Scorpion Bite THEN

    // Symptomatic Treatment
    IF pain is present THEN
        ADMINISTER simple analgesics such as paracetamol OR aspirin.
    ENDIF

    // Urgent Referral
    MAKE EVERY EFFORT to transport the patient to a hospital as soon as possible due to potential for severe reactions.

    // Hospital-Based Treatment (within 24 hours of attack)
    IF patient is in a hospital setting THEN
        ADMINISTER vasodilators.
        // Note: Vasodilators may attenuate cardiovascular response and possibly reduce mortality.
    ENDIF

    // Antivenom Therapy (in endemic area, if available)
    IF patient is in an endemic area AND species-specific antiscorpion serum is available locally THEN
        ADMINISTER antiscorpion serum within a few hours of the attack.
    ENDIF

ENDIF
