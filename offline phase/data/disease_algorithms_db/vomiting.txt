```text
DISEASE: Vomiting

ABBREVIATIONS:
- IV: Intravenous
- IM: Intramuscular
- NSAIDS: Nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs
- Inj.: Injection
- Tab.: Tablet
- mg: Milligram
- OD: Once daily
- BD: Twice daily
- TDS: Three times daily
- QDS: Four times daily
- PRN: As needed
- PO: Per os (by mouth)

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Clinical presentation: Forceful expulsion of gastric contents.
- History taking: Assess duration, frequency, associated symptoms (pain, dehydration), potential causes (motion sickness, pregnancy, medication side effects, etc.).
- Physical examination: Assess hydration status, abdominal examination.
- Investigations (if needed): Rule out gastric outlet obstruction, consider endoscopy if symptoms persist.

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF patient is dehydrated THEN
    Hospitalize the patient.
    Administer intravenous fluids.
    Start oral fluids as soon as the patient can tolerate them.
    Administer appropriate analgesics if the patient has severe pain.
ELSE
    Encourage oral fluids.
    Administer appropriate analgesics if the patient has severe pain.
ENDIF

IF acute vomiting THEN
    Rule out gastric outlet obstruction.
    IF gastric outlet obstruction is ruled out THEN
        Administer Inj. ondansetron 8 mg IV repeat every 8 hours if needed.
        OR
        Administer Inj. metoclopramide 10 mg IM, repeat after 6 hours if needed.
        OR
        Administer Tab. domperidone 10 mg three times a day (TDS).
        OR
        Administer Tab. metoclopramide 10 mg three times a day (TDS).
    ENDIF
ENDIF

IF patient is pregnant THEN
    AVOID all drugs if possible.
    IF patient is in the first trimester THEN
        Administer Tab. promethazine 25 mg oral/injection.
    ENDIF
ENDIF

IF patient has a history of motion sickness THEN
    Administer Tab. cyclizine 50 mg three times a day (TDS).
ENDIF

PATIENT EDUCATION:
- Avoid stale food, cut vegetables, fruits kept in open.
- Drink potable water only.
- Avoid NSAIDS, especially if ulcer symptoms are present.
- Prevent dehydration: Encourage patients to take sips of liquids at short intervals.
- Endoscopy is necessary if symptoms persist.
- Prevent motion sickness by avoiding heavy meals before travel.
```
