DISEASE: Giardiasis

ABBREVIATIONS:
- TDS: Three times daily
- mg/kg: Milligrams per kilogram
- g: Grams

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Demonstration of cysts and/or trophozoites in the stools.

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:
IF Patient is an adult
  THEN
    IF Treatment choice is tinidazole
      THEN Administer tinidazole 2 g as a single dose.
    ELSE IF Treatment choice is metronidazole
      THEN Administer metronidazole 400 mg every TDS for 7 days.
  ELSE
    IF Treatment choice is tinidazole
      THEN Administer tinidazole 50 mg/kg as a single dose.
    ELSE IF Treatment choice is metronidazole
      THEN Administer metronidazole 15 mg/kg divided in three doses for 7 days.
ENDIF

- (None specified in the provided text for tinidazole or metronidazole regarding giardiasis treatment, although a disulfiram-like reaction with alcohol is noted).
