```text
DISEASE: Megaloblastic Anemia

ABBREVIATIONS:
- mcg: microgram
- mg: milligram
- Hb: Hemoglobin
- RBC: Red Blood Cell
- IM: Intramuscular

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Detailed diet history
- Hb concentration
- Reticulocyte count
- RBC indices

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF Folic Acid Deficiency is diagnosed THEN
    IF Patient age is < 6 months THEN
        Administer Oral folic acid at a dose of 15 mcg/kg for 3 months.
    ELSE IF Patient age is between 7 months and 13 years (inclusive) THEN
        Administer Oral folic acid at a dose of 1 mg for 3 weeks.
        THEN Administer Oral folic acid at a dose of 0.1-0.5 mg for the next 3 months.
    ENDIF
ELSE IF Vitamin B12 Deficiency is diagnosed THEN
    IF Oral/sublingual vitamin B12 is chosen THEN
        Administer Oral/sublingual vitamin B12 at a dose of 500-1000 mcg/day for 4-6 weeks.
        THEN Administer Oral/sublingual vitamin B12 at a dose of 25-50 mcg/day for the next 3 months.
    ELSE IF Parenteral vitamin B12 is chosen THEN
        Administer Parenteral vitamin B12 at a dose of 100 mcg IM for 2 weeks.
        THEN Administer Parenteral vitamin B12 at a dose of 100-250 mcg/day IM until complete correction.
    ENDIF
ENDIF
```
