DISEASE: Peptic Ulcer Disease

ABBREVIATIONS:
- NSAIDs: Nonsteroidal Anti-inflammatory Drugs
- PPI: Proton Pump Inhibitor
- H.pylori: Helicobacter pylori
- QID: Four times a day
- BD: Twice a day
- OD: Once a day
- mg: Milligrams
- gm: Gram

DIAGNOSTICS:
- Ulceration of gastric or duodenal mucosa due to acid and pepsin.
- Symptoms: Heartburn, relation with foods, upper abdominal pain, weight loss, anorexia, cachexia (in advanced cases).
- History: NSAIDs or steroid ingestion.
- Perforation: Severe abdominal pain, guarding, rigidity, shock.

TREATMENT ALGORITHM:

IF Patient is on long term NSAIDs OR has bleeding peptic ulcer THEN
    IF Eradicating H.pylori is indicated THEN
        IF Using Preferred Regimen (Table 1) THEN
            IF Drug is Bismuth subsalicylate THEN
                Administer 2 tab QID for Two Week
            ELSE IF Drug is Tetracycline THEN
                Administer 250 mg QID for Two Week
            ELSE IF Drug is Metronidazole (first regimen) THEN
                Administer 500 mg QID for Two Week
            ELSE IF Drug is Bismuth citrate THEN
                Administer 400 mg BD for Two Week
            ELSE IF Drug is Amoxicillin (first regimen) THEN
                Administer 500 mg BD for Two Week
            ELSE IF Drug is Metronidazole (second regimen) THEN
                Administer 500 mg BD for Two Week
            ELSE IF Drug is Omeprazole THEN
                Administer 20 mg BD for Two Week
            ELSE IF Drug is Amoxicillin (second regimen) THEN
                Administer 1 gm BD for Two Week
            ELSE IF Drug is Clarithromycin THEN
                Administer 500 mg BD for Two Week
            ENDIF
        ENDIF
        Administer Proton Pump Inhibitor (PPI) for 3 weeks after triple therapy.
    ELSE
        IF Non-H.pylori peptic ulcer THEN
            Administer any PPI for 4 to 6 weeks, 45 minutes before breakfast.
            OR
            Administer Tab. ranitidine 150 mg BD.
            OR
            Administer Tab. famotidine 40 mg OD.
        ENDIF
    ENDIF
ELSE

    ENDIF
ENDIF
